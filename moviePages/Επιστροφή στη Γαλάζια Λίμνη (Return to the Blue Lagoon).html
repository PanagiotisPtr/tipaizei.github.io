Επιστροφή στη Γαλάζια Λίμνη (Return to the Blue Lagoon)


Rating: (5.1/10) | Duration: 1h 42min | Start Time: 15:00



Decription:
In this sequel to Η γαλάζια λίμνη (1980), two children are stranded on a beautiful island in the South Pacific. With no adults to guide them, the two make a simple life together and eventually become suntanned teenagers in love.
